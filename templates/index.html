What is the MBTI?

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is a personality assessment that categorizes individuals into 16 distinct personality types based on four key dichotomies:

Extraversion (E) vs Introversion (I)

How you interact with the world

Sensing (S) vs Intuition (N)

How you process information

Thinking (T) vs Feeling (F)

How you make decisions

Judging (J) vs Perceiving (P)

How you organize your life
